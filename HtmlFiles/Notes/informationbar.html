Information Bar

To open the information bar, click on the top right button next to the light/dark mode button. Inside are the ACLED Codebook box and the Data tables box.

ACLED Codebook

The data that ACLED collects is quite nuanced. ACLED has developed a codebook to help people understand their dataset. Event definitions and variables information is contained here. The codebook box below are selected excerpts from their official codebook. For the full codebook https://acleddata.com/acleddatanew/wp-content/uploads/2021/11/ACLED_Codebook_v1_January-2021.pdf and for more information go to their website at https://www.acleddata.com

Data tables

View the selected data that is being displayed in the various boxes in the dashboard and also the processed data.
